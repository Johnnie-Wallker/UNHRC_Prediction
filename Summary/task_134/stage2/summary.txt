3. **Diverse Professional Backgrounds**: Candidates come from varied professional backgrounds, including non-governmental organizations (NGOs), academia, and the corporate sector. 4. **Relevant Current Employment**: The successful candidates are currently engaged in roles that are directly or indirectly related to their field of expertise, such as legal practice, academic research, or NGO work. This indicates their active involvement and continued relevance in their respective fields.

5. **Geographical Representation**: The candidates represent different countries within the Central and South American, and Caribbean region, ensuring a broad geographical representation that aligns with the mandate to address indigenous rights across these diverse areas.
